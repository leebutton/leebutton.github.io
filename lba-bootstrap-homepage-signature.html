The approach

The Horizon Method

I use the Horizon Method – my unique methodology. A practical, workshop-based approach, it’s designed to define purpose, assess alignment, and identify how to facilitate change. It’s consistent, collaborative and highly effective.

Step 1

Clarify

Define purpose, priorities and boundaries.

Step 2

Align

Bring leaders and teams around a small number of shared decisions.

Step 3

Act

Translate those decisions into practical action and review progress regularly.
