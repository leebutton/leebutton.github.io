Weymouth, Portland and Dorset

Help, support and a safe place to be heard.

The Lantern Trust supports people facing homelessness, housing problems, financial hardship, mental health difficulties, addiction, isolation and crisis. We listen first, then help people find the right next step.

Nowhere safe to stay tonight?

If you are in immediate danger or at risk of violence, call 999. If you have nowhere to stay outside normal working hours, contact Dorset Council’s emergency out-of-hours service on 01305 221000. During Lantern opening hours, contact us or visit the drop-in and we will help you understand your options.
